I needed a tool to document my film scans so that I could later come back and see what camera, lens, developer was used in the process. I came up with this tool which was originally writen in C# for Windows but after I migrated to Ubuntu I needed a tool that worked on Linux so I re-wrote FilmScanData in Electron which is cross platform.

FilmScanData is free but if you find it useful consider donating a dollar or two which will help with future releases and development of other tools that are in the works.
